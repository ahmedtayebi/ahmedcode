Med Ouargla DZ

مرجعك الطبي الشامل | جامعة ورقلة

Medical students in Ouargla University lack a centralized, reliable platform for academic resources. Existing solutions are scattered across WhatsApp groups and outdated forums, making it hard to find trustworthy study materials.

Solution:
